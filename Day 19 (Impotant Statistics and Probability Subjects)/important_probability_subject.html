Introduction to Probability

Definition : Probability is a mathematical term that measures the likelihood of an event's occuring

0 : Impossible to occur

1 : Certain to occur

0.5 : Unlikely

$$ P(A) = \frac{\text{Number of wanted occasions}}{\text{Number of all possible occasions}} $$

Probability Rules

Rule Formula Example
Addition Rule
(Mutually Exclusive)		 P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) Rolling a die and getting a 2 or a 5
Addition Rule
(Non-Mutually Exclusive)		 P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) − P(A ∩ B) Drawing a Heart or a Face card from a deck
Multiplication Rule
(Independent Events)		 P(A ∩ B) = P(A) × P(B) Flipping a coin and rolling a die
Multiplication Rule
(Dependent Events)		 P(A ∩ B) = P(A) × P(B|A) Drawing two Aces from a deck (without replacement)
